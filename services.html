Services

Accidents happen. So does life. That's why PGMC is here for you every day.
Our round-the-clock emergency room is close to home, with doctors and nurses who are here when you need them.
Our highly skilled staff treats both children and adults in a caring, family-friendly environment.
We offer state-of-the-art diagnostic and laboratory services, including X-ray, CT, MRI and ultrasound.
All of which means no matter what the emergency – from stitches to stroke – you can count on our rapid response, personalized care and medical resources.

PGMC Nursing Outpatient Services provides treatment for a wide variety of medical conditions. Available 24/7, our compassionate caregivers are here to care for you and your loved ones.
Our experienced nurses play an integral role in coordinating your care with physicians and clinical pharmacists, while working to achieve the best possible outcome.
For your convenience, we schedule your services prior to arrival, avoiding an ER visit and the need to wait for your medications.

Infusion services include:
-IV medications including antibiotics
-Subcutaneous and intramuscular medications
-blood product transfusion

PGMC surgery team provides you with the best experience possible—before, during and after surgery. We strive to make every patient feel comfortable and keep them well informed.
Our staff takes the time to make sure there is an understanding between the patient, the nurse and the team of doctors.
We provide comprehensive general surgery services and specialty skills in orthopedic, pain management and podiatry, as well as infusion services. We also do colonoscopy and endoscopy screenings. All surgeons are qualified in their fields and have the training and experience to care for a wide-variety of surgical problems.
All members of our team are dedicated to providing you with quality surgical care tailored to the specific needs of your case.

PGMC Hospital’s laboratory is available around-the-clock for emergency patients, which means you receive your test results when you need them. For non-emergencies, see our lab hours below.
Skyline’s in-house testing provides a quick, accurate ability to diagnose infections. Our CLIA-certified lab works closely with the Klickitat County Health Department and local businesses in need of testing. Tests are done on a drop in and confidential basis. Employee drug testing services are also available.
