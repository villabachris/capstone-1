About

Healthcare facility in Quezon City. Is a modern Level 3 (Tertiary) Hospital with 100 bed capacity which will be housed in a dedicated Patient Care Wing, located along Mindanao Avenue near Quirino Hi-way in Novaliches, Quezon City.

Mission

At PGMC, our mission is to improve the health of those we serve with a commitment to excellence in all that we do. Our goal is to offer quality care and services that set community standards, exceed patients' expectations and are provided in a caring, convenient, cost-effective and accessible manner.

Vision

To provide each patient with the world-class care, exceptional service and compassion we would want for our loved ones.
